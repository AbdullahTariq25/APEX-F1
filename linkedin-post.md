I just finished and deployed my latest frontend project: **APEX F1** 🏎️💨

It's a high-performance, dark-themed web experience inspired by Formula 1 engineering. I really wanted to challenge myself by building an "Apple-style" website where a 3D image sequence is perfectly synced to your scroll position.

🛠️ **Built with:** Next.js, Tailwind CSS V4, GSAP, Framer Motion, and Lenis for buttery-smooth scrolling.

Syncing complex animations to precise scroll progress was tough but absolutely worth it! You can experience the momentum-based scrolling firsthand in the live demo.

🌐 Live Demo: https://car-formula.vercel.app/
💻 GitHub Repo: https://github.com/AbdullahTariq25/APEX-F1.git
